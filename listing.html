Terms of Service

PointSwap is a coordination platform only. We do not process payments or guarantee transactions. All exchanges are your own responsibility.

By creating an account you agree that:

  • You are a currently enrolled university student with a valid .edu email.
  • All meal exchanges take place in person at a campus dining hall.
  • You will only post listings for dining points you genuinely have available.
  • You will never pay in advance — always wait until the meal is purchased in front of you.
  • You will not use PointSwap to deceive, defraud, or harm other users.

Fraud & Scams:

Fraudulent activity is entirely the responsibility of the individual committing it. PointSwap bears no liability for scams, fraud, no-shows, or any dishonest conduct. Fraudulent users may be reported to university administration.

No Guarantees:

PointSwap makes no guarantees about the availability or legitimacy of listings, whether sellers have the points they claim, or whether any exchange will take place. You use PointSwap entirely at your own risk.

Limitation of Liability:

PointSwap and its creators are not liable for any financial loss, personal injury, or damages of any kind arising from exchanges arranged through the platform.
