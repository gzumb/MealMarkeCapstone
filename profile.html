PointSwap is a UMass Boston student platform for sharing unused dining points. Sellers convert expiring points into value. Buyers get affordable meals. Everyone wins.

1
Post or find a meal
Sellers post their available dining points with a discounted price. Buyers browse and filter by location and time.
2
Request and coordinate
Send a request to the seller. Once accepted, use the built-in chat to agree on a meetup time.
3
Meet at the dining hall
The seller swipes their points. The buyer pays the agreed amount in cash or Venmo. Done.

Step 2 of 4

A few ground rules to keep PointSwap safe and fair for everyone.

All exchanges happen in person at a campus dining hall. Never share payment details online.
Only verified UMass Boston students can use this platform. Do not share your account.
Prices must be discounted — the whole point is saving money. Don't list at or above face value.
Rate every exchange — ratings keep the community trustworthy for everyone.
Cancel listings you can no longer fulfill — don't leave buyers hanging.
